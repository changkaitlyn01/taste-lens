# TasteLens — AI Review Insight Extractor

## Overview
TasteLens is a lightweight AI workflow that converts unstructured restaurant reviews into structured value-driver insights. It extracts recurring praise themes, complaint patterns, vibe signals, and differentiators to help users quickly understand why people actually like a place.

## Problem
Restaurant reviews are noisy and repetitive. Users must read many reviews to understand true strengths and weaknesses.

## Approach
- Sample public restaurant reviews
- Define structured insight schema
- Use AI extraction prompts
- Convert review text into categorized signals

## Insight Categories
- Praise Drivers
- Complaint Drivers
- Vibe & Experience Signals
- Occasion Fit
- Differentiators

## Status
Prototype in progress — prompt-based extraction workflow.
